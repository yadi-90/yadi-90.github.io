Hola! My name is Yadira Morales, and I'm originally from the beautiful city of Ensenada, Mexico, where the fish taco was invented. I'm a mother of three kids and live with my husband here in Utah. My goal is to inspire others, especially women, to see the power in their own lives, pursue their goals. I want my work to make a difference in the world, whether it's through new discoveries or connections.
